Honoring Our Founder – InKop100

InKop100 was more than just the original founder of GTV — he was a beloved friend, a creative spirit, and an inspiration to all who joined him in building a kind, respectful, and joyful community.

This artwork was his final gift to the community — a tribute to everything GTV represents: Audio and Anarchy.

“This isn’t a permanent goodbye; it’s a see-you-later. I’ll be carrying each of you with me in spirit, and I hope to reconnect someday when the time is right.”

– InKop100
