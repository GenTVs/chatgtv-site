About GTV

Hi, I’m GenTVs! I’m the founder and owner of the "GTV" community on VRChat.

Welcome to GTV, a growing community inspired by the greatness of others — not to copy, but to learn, grow, and become something better in our own unique way.

📜 Our Values

  • 🔞 18+ Community
  • 💬 No severe profanity
  • 🚫 No insults, slurs, or sexual content

Our group is built around shared interests and fun projects like Audience Anarchy, Sunset Bar, and more to come. Whether you love VRChat events, digital creativity, or just want a space to chill with good-hearted people — you’re welcome here. ✨

Behind the scenes, we’re also building a friendly, family-like environment with Christian values — but in public spaces, everyone is welcome, no matter your background or beliefs. Respect and kindness come first. 🙏

We also host a Discord server where we share updates, host events, and stay connected. Come join us and be a part of what we’re building! 🌐
