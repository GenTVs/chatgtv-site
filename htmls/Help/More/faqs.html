Community FAQ

All your community questions answered in one place.

What are the rules in GTV?

Please refer to our Community Guidelines.

Do I need VR to use VRChat?

Nope! You can use Desktop Mode — all you need is a mouse, keyboard, and screen. It works like any first-person PC game. Just install from Steam and select "Launch in Desktop Mode."

Is the GTV Community age-restricted?

Yes. GTV is a strictly 18+ community. Please verify before participating in our events or joining our Discord.

Can I join GTV without a headset?

Yes! Most members hang out in both VR and Desktop. You’ll be welcome no matter how you log in.

Where can I find more info?

Explore our website or check out Contact Us to reach out directly.
