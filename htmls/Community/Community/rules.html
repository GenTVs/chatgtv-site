Community Rules

Updated: June 5, 2025

These rules ensure a respectful and safe environment for all GTV members. By joining the GTV community, you agree to uphold these expectations:

  • 18+ only — This community is intended strictly for adults.
  • No severe profanity — Keep language respectful and considerate.
  • No insults & slurs — Any form of hate speech is strictly prohibited.
  • No sexual content — Keep all interactions clean and community-safe.

For any questions or concerns regarding our rules, please contact a staff member or reach out via the Help section.
