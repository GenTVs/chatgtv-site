ChatGTV and chatgtv.io

GTV Update 0.3.0

We're proud to announce the release of GTV Update 0.3.0 — our most important platform update to date! This marks the beginning of a tighter integration between our newly structured chatgtv.io website and our beloved AI assistant, ChatGTV.

🔧 Major Improvements

This update brings a unified experience for both visitors and community members by enabling real-time content reading for ChatGTV. It now actively references the live content of the site to answer user questions more accurately and with current context.

🧠 ChatGTV Just Got Smarter

ChatGTV can now directly read public GTV content, including:

  • The Founder
  • About Us
  • Staff Team
  • Ranking System
  • GTV Blog
  • Rules
  • Guidelines
  • Schedule
  • FAQs

These are parsed regularly via ChatGTV’s local index.js integration running on your local machine (typically inside C:\Windows\System32\ChatGTV), so make sure your deployment is active.

⏱️ Live Time Sync

We’ve also added a hidden page for real-time synchronization: chatgtv.io/ChatGTV ONLY/time.html. This allows ChatGTV to give current time-based replies in sync with German time zones, ensuring better coordination for announcements or events.

🌐 Website Polish

Besides technical improvements, we gave the site a full refresh:

  • New Open Graph and Twitter Card previews
  • Brand new Favicon set and icon identity
  • Updated Hero sections with Grow – Shine – Connect layout
  • Discord-friendly blog embed previews

Even pages like Blog now show beautiful thumbnails when shared on Discord or Twitter.

🚧 Unofficial Release Status

This version is considered an Unofficial Release. That means:

  • Some pages (like Schedule or Contact Us) are still under construction
  • No external contact forms or help desks exist yet
  • Media content (like Photos and Videos) is still being prepared

We recommend contacting the GTV team directly via Discord until official support options go live.

💬 Final Words

We’re beyond excited about this new chapter for GTV. ChatGTV is no longer just a support bot — it’s now our assistant, librarian, and soon-to-be guide for everything GTV. There’s still more to come. And we’ll build it with you.

– The GTV Team
