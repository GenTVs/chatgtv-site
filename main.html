Who Are We?

GTV is a VRChat community that brings together passionate explorers, creators, and leaders. We're all about fun, creativity, and respectful friendship.

What Do We Do?

We host events, build worlds, support new users, and offer a space where everyone feels welcomed. Whether you're joining a hangout night or learning Unity, we're with you.

Why GTV?

Because we believe in creating a positive, inclusive environment. From our volunteer staff to our uplifting messages, we aim to be a home for VR adventurers.

Upcoming Events

Join us weekly for game nights, special VR meetups, seasonal events, and competitions. Want to help host one? Join our team!
